Dataset: The German credit score (AIF360, OpenML dataset 31)
URL Dataset: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/144/statlog+german+credit+data

For this project we use the German Credit dataset to develop a predictive model designed to simulate a scoring scenario. 
We aim to demonstrate how a model can be created in favor of specific demographics while maintaining a superficial 
appearance of fairness. By applying Explainable AI (XAI) techniques, we will then expose the latent biases tied to sensitive characteristics.
